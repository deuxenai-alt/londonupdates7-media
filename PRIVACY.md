# Privacy Policy — Food Science UK Poster

This application is a personal publishing tool used solely by its owner to
schedule and publish posts to their own LinkedIn company page (Food Science UK).

- It does not collect, store, or share any personal data from any third party.
- It accesses only the owner's own LinkedIn account and company page, via
  LinkedIn's official API, with the owner's explicit authorization.
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
- Authorization can be revoked at any time from LinkedIn's settings.
