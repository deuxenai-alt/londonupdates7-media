# 🏔️ Peak District Road Trip Guide — 6 Mates, One Car

An interactive, budget-first digital guide for a Peak District road trip from London for a group of 6 (you + 5 friends), sharing one car.

**Built for the @londonupdates7 niche** (UK facts / lifestyle / weekend escapes).

## What's inside

- 🗺️ **Interactive map** (Leaflet + OpenStreetMap) with 24 pin-dropped spots
  - 🟣 Attractions & viewpoints · 🟢 Free parking · 🟡 Cheap/paid parking · 🔴 Food
  - Category filters + one-tap **Google Maps directions** on every pin
- 💷 **Full costed budget** — day trip (~£50pp) and weekend (~£136pp), July 2026 prices
- 📅 **Seasonal guide** — what to catch and when (⭐ = go-now highlights)
- 🅿️ **Free & cheap parking** list (13 of the park's car parks are free)
- 🍽️ **Cheap eats & pubs** — Grindleford Station Café, Castleton, Bakewell & more
- 🕒 **Suggested 2-day itinerary**

## View it

Open `index.html` in any browser, or host it free on **GitHub Pages**:

### Enable GitHub Pages (2 minutes)
1. Push this branch (already done).
2. On GitHub: **Settings → Pages**.
3. Under *Build and deployment → Source*, pick **Deploy from a branch**.
4. Branch: `claude/peak-dt-road-trip-5g5pd5`, folder: `/ (root)` → **Save**.
5. Your guide goes live at:
   `https://deuxenai-alt.github.io/londonupdates7-media/peak-district-road-trip/`

> Tip: once you're happy, merge to `main` and serve Pages from `main` so the URL is permanent.
