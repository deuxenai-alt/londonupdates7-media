Keep the wallet shut
Free & cheap parking

🟢 Free

  • Mam Tor roadside — laybys on Old Mam Tor Rd
  • Hurst Clough — ~15 spaces, Upper Derwent
  • Upperdale — ~10 spaces, Monsal Trail
  • A6013 laybys — for Ladybower walks

🟡 Cheap

  • Fairholmes — hub for Ladybower + café
  • Bakewell Ag. Centre — big & central
  • Hook's Car — closest to Stanage Edge
  • Dovedale NT — free for NT members
